TX16W_S
Saves samples for the Yamaha TX16W.
Loops yes (forward)
Channels no (mono)
Compressionno (PCM-12)
These samples are always 12-bit, are limited in length to 262144 samples (attack- and sustainpart) and supporting only three different rates (16 kHz, 33 kHz, 50 kHz).
