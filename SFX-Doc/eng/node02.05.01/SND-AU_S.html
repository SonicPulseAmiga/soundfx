SND-AU_S
Saves SND-AU samples.
Loops no
Channels yes (mono/stereo/quadro)
Compressionyes (PCM-8,PCM-16,PCM-24,PCM-32,A-LAW,µ-LAW,IEEE-32,IEEE-64)
These samples come mainly from the SUN, NEXT or DEC computers or in common : most UNIX-based machines are using this . The is pretty simple - a small header followed by the sound data. In most cases these are µ-Law packed.

Parameter
Typetype of compression
  • PCM8 : not compressed 8bit
  • PCM16 : not compressed 16bit
  • PCM24 : not compressed 24bit
  • PCM32 : not compressed 32bit
  • µ-Law : µ-Law (14:8) compressed 14bit
  • A-Law : A-Law (14:8) compressed 14bit
  • IEEE-32 : floating point 32bit
  • IEEE-64 : floating point 64bit
Hdr Fileheader
  • SND : SUN's
  • DEC : DEC-workstation's
  • I_SND,I_DEC : PC with UNIX (LINUX)

Notes
