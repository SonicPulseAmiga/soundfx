1.5 Windows

Many menu-entries and toolbar-buttons are followed by dialog-windows.

You will probably note, that none of the SoundFX windows have cancel-buttons. I have left them out, as you can achieve the same results but closing the window with the close-gadget in the upper left corner of the window-frame or by pressing the ESC-key. Similar to that pressing the ENTER or RETURN key you select the okay-button (marked in bold) of that window.
