QuantizeHoriz
"Holds" the sample values for a given time.

Parameter
Effect (P1)how much the operator effects the outcome
Quantisation Range (P2)how long a sample value is supposed to be held

Notes
This effect gives a sample a "Nintendo" sound. It's also well known as Sample&Hold.
