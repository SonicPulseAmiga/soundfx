AmplifySplit
Allows independet adjustment of volume for upper and lower parts of a sample. Replaces Clap and Clear operators from older versions of SoundFX.

Parameter
Upper Amplification (P1)This value controls the amount of amplification for the upper sample-half.
Lower Amplification (P2)This value controls the amount of amplification for the lower sample-half.
MaxVolUpperAfter a clicking this button, the current source will be scanned and the maximum amplification without clipping the upper values will be calculated.
MaxVolLowerAfter a clicking this button, the current source will be scanned and the maximum amplification without clipping the lower values will be calculated.

Notes
see Amplify operator
