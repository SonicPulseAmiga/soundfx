VOC_L
Loads SoundBlaster-VOC samples.
Loops yes (forward)
Channels yes (mono/stereo/quadro)
Compressionyes (PCM-8,PCM-16,ADPCM-8:4,ADPCM-8:3,ADPCM-8:2,A-LAW,µ-LAW)
The VOC was introduced by "Creative Labs", creators of the Soundblaster-cards on the PC. It was created for easy playback from disks and hard disks or CDs giving it a host of advantages. However due to inconsequent planning it became nessecary to 'add' features which slow down handling of this . Most programs aren't able to read but one (the 1.1 version) of the VOC . SoundFX can read and write all known versions of this .
