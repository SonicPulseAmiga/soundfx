1.5.7 status window

In this window the progress of an operation will be shown. Therefore SoundFX utilizes a growing status-bar with a percentage-display. Additional information is given in the title of the window.

The calculation can be stopped with one click at "Stop", pressing the keys "S","s","ESC" or a click at the "Close"-gadget of the window.
